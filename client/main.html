Welcome to Project NORTHPAW!

It stands for "Narrative Online Roleplaying something something Powered by Apocalypse World".

It's a storytelling, roleplaying game, played over the internet. The players don't all have to be online at the same time, so it'll fit into busy schedules like a play-by-post game would - you can log in and take a few actions whenever you can. But also it'll use the Apocalypse World engine to produce powerful, human, dramatic stories - something which can be difficult to achieve in play-by-post.

At the moment, though, there's nothing here. See the Project Northpaw Twitter feed for a bit more detail on what's happening and where we're going. (Only very irregular updates, though - I wouldn't bother following the account. It's just a convenient place to host short updates.)
