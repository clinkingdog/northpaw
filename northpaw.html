Welcome to Project NORTHPAW!

It stands for "Narrative Online Roleplaying something something Powered by Apocalypse World".

It's a storytelling, roleplaying game, played in turns over the internet. I'm hoping it'll fit into busy schedules like a play-by-post game would, so you can log in and take a few actions whenever you have time - but I'm also hoping it'll also support the powerful, human, dramatic stories of games like Apocalypse World, which is something I haven't experienced in most play-by-post games.

At the moment, though, there's nothing here.
